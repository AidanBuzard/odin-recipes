Pizza Recipe

Description

This is the recipe to make a delicious yet simple pepperoni pizza for a group of people to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pepperonis

Steps

  1. Spread flour onto pizza dough and shape to a good diameter
  2. Spread marinara sauce evenly across the dough in a circular pattern
  3. Place cheese evenly across the the marinara sauce
  4. Place pepperonis across the cheese
  5. Heat in an oven at 365 degrees for about 10-15 minutes
  6. Enjoy
