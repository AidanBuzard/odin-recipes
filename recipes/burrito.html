Burrito Recipe

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a tasty and simple burrito with few ingredients! Once you follow this recipe once, it is so easy to replicate it and add your own flare!

Ingredients

  • Can of refried beans
  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Optional: Guacamole

Steps

  1. Start cooking your choice of either ground beef or chicken
  2. Start cooking your refried beans
  3. Make sure to check on meat while it cooks
  4. Lay out other ingredients to be served on your counter
  5. Once meat of choice, and refried beans are all cooked, tell your guests to grab a tortilla and put whatever ingredients they want for their own taste
