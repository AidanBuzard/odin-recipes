Quesidilla Recipe

Description

This is the recipe for a very simple quesidilla to snack on.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream or Guacamole
  • Optional: Meat and peppers

Steps

  1. Grab two tortillas and put one on a heated pan
  2. Put the cheese of your choice on the tortilla, covering the surface
  3. Put second tortilla on top
  4. Evenly flip the tortillas to get a crispt shell
  5. Take out when cheese is fully metled
  6. Dip it into sour cream or Guacamole and enjoy
